# Thought: DIFFICULTY
> [!Thought-en]
> There is nothing harder in life than being a Christian—but it’s NOT because Christianity is hard!
> It’s because I AM HARD: hard-hearted, hard-headed and hard of hearing!! 
> I need to ask Jesus to SOFTEN ME UP!!!
