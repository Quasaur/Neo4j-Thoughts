# Thought: THE REAL YOU
> [!Thought-en]
> Dear Christian: your earthly self is just an avatar.
> Your Real Self is hid with Christ in GOD; that’s why Jesus MUST return: so that you (and everyone else) can behold who you really are! 
> Colossians 3:1-4
