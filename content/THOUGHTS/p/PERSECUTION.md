# Thought: PERSECUTION
> [!Thought-en]
> In most of the world Christianity is persecuted vigorously.
> In countries where it is NOT persecuted, the reason is because THAT VERSION OF CHRISTIANITY IS NOT WORTH PERSECUTING.
